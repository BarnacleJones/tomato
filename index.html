Tomato Timer

This is a productivity timer that follows the pomodoro technique

Follow this order

  1. 25 Minutes (productivity)
  2. 5 Minutes (break)
  3. 25 Minutes (productivity)
  4. 5 Minutes (break)
  5. 25 Minutes (productivity)
  6. 5 Minutes (break)
  7. 25 Minutes (productivity)
  8. 10 Minutes (break)
  9. Restart!
